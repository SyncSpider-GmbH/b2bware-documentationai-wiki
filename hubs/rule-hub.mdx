## Overview

RuleHub is DataHub's pricing-and-promotions engine. It evaluates business rules against a cart, a product catalog, or a shipping context and produces price adjustments, validation failures, or shipping rates.

It exposes five rule families plus coupons, all backed by a shared condition schema.

**Base URL:** `api/v1/apps/rule-hub/`

<Callout kind="info">
Storefront clients (the `shop` role) get read-only access to rules and coupons â€” enough to render cart pricing and promotional badges. Write operations are admin-only.
</Callout>

## Rule Families

| Family | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Catalog Price Rules** | Adjust list/final price on products that match the condition (e.g. "10% off all `category=accessories` for B2B group"). Applied at catalog read time. |
| **Cart Price Rules** | Apply discounts at the cart level (e.g. "spend â‚¬100, get â‚¬10 off", BOGO, fixed cart discount). |
| **Cart Validation Rules** | Block checkout unless constraints hold (e.g. min order value, max quantity per SKU, restricted shipping country). |
| **Shipping Rate Rules** | Compute shipping cost for a cart from zone, weight, value, and conditions. |
| **Coupons** | Codes that activate one or more cart price rules. |
